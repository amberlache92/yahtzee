Yahtzee rules

Objective

This is a game of stratedgy and chance. You goal is to get the highest score from 5 dice. There are 13 rounds. In each round you are allowed up to 3 rolls enabling you to reroll any of the die again.Each category scores differently.At the end of the game your score is then calculated

Upper section

Your scores in this section is the total of the die chosen. For example if you roll 5 -2-3-5-5 you can choose to add up all the fives or twos or threes. If you choose any other category that does not have a possible score in them you will recieve a zero in that space.

You receive a bounus of 35 points if your upper section scores at least a 63.

Lower Section

In the lower section you eithr get a set amount or azero if you do not have any other possible scores

  • 3 and 4 of a kind, for 3 of a kind you must have a least 3 die faces taht are the same. For 4 of a kind you must have at least 4 die faces be the same.
  • small straight, is a sequence of at least 4 die faces in a row. Example 2-3-4-5< for a score of 30.
  • large straight, is a sequence of a least 5 die faces in a row.Example 1-2-3-4-5 for a score of 40.
  • Full house, is when you have a 3 of a kind and 2 of a kind. Example 1-1-2-2-2 for a score of 25
  • Yahtzee, is 5 of a kind for a score of 50 points. Unlike most games there are no bonus yahtzees.
  • Chance, is basically a free category where the values of all the die are added up for points.
